About

The James Joyce management and staff would like to introduce and welcome you to "Baltimore's Home of Irish Hospitality". As Harbor East blossoms and grows, we are the place where local residents and business people can come to relax. Our cozy, inviting atmosphere is the ideal place to sit back and enjoy a pint with an authentic hearty meal. The James Joyce is a traditional Irish Pub and Restaurant, which was designed and built in Ireland and shipped over in the fall of 2002, where it was then fitted on site. Named after the famous Irish author and poet, our pub offers fast and friendly service. Conveniently located at the end of Interstate 83 South we are the ideal place to drop off your tour guests for a lovely meal. Arrangements have been made for any busses that will need to park during the visit. The surrounding area is rich in history and culture, with plenty to see. Once inside the restaurant, you will be transported across miles of ocean, to a charming and inviting family setting. Whether you are pulling up a barstool and engaging in conversation with your co-workers; or pulling up a chair at a happy family gathering and being warmed by the fire, we have whatever is needed to make you next tour a special one.
